StoreMessage is called by the client, and serializes the received message into the SensorInfoContainer.

A received message usually corresponds to one individual SensorInfo object. Depending on your data, it can be necessary to timestamp the message using System.DateTime.Now, or to serialize multiple SensorInfo objects from a single message.
